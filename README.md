# PROSPERA GROWTH

## Mission

Helping Local Businesses Grow.

## Business Growth System

This repository contains the official PROSPERA GROWTH Business Growth System.
It is designed to help local businesses move from initial discovery to a clear
growth plan, implementation, and measurable tracking.

The Business Discovery Form is only Phase 1 of the complete Business Growth
System.

## System Phases

### Phase 1

Business Discovery

### Phase 2

Business Analysis

### Phase 3

Growth Blueprint

### Phase 4

Implementation

### Phase 5

Growth Tracking

## Growth Capabilities

This repository contains reusable systems for local business growth including:

- Business Discovery
- Marketing
- Social Media
- CRM
- Lead Generation
- Follow-up Systems
- Reporting
- Client Assets

## Project Structure

Every client must have their own folder under:

```text
clients/
```

Use this format:

```text
001-ClientName
002-ClientName
003-ClientName
```

## Official Client Folders

Each client folder should keep work organized by phase and asset type:

```text
discovery/
website/
social-media/
marketing/
reports/
assets/
branding/
README.md
```

## Reusable Growth System

The Business Discovery Form is the official Phase 1 intake asset for all future
clients. It is part of the broader reusable Business Growth System and should be
used to gather the information needed for analysis, strategy, implementation,
and tracking.

New client work should start by copying the Phase 1 discovery structure into
that client's own folder, then continuing through the remaining growth phases as
the engagement progresses.

Current client implementation:

```text
clients/001-Jordan-Came-to-Serve-Home-Services/website/
```

System documentation:

```text
docs/business-growth-system.md
```

Template guidance:

```text
templates/business-discovery-form/
```
