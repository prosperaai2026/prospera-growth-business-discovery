# PROSPERA GROWTH Business Growth System

## Positioning

PROSPERA GROWTH helps local businesses grow through a structured growth process,
not a one-time form.

Mission:

```text
Helping Local Businesses Grow.
```

## The Complete Process

The Business Growth System is organized into five phases.

## Phase 1: Business Discovery

Collect the client's business information, current process, service areas,
customer sources, challenges, goals, and growth opportunities.

Primary asset:

```text
Business Discovery Form
```

## Phase 2: Business Analysis

Review the discovery answers and identify gaps, strengths, bottlenecks,
positioning opportunities, marketing issues, communication problems, and growth
constraints.

## Phase 3: Growth Blueprint

Create a practical growth plan for the client. This may include marketing
strategy, service positioning, lead generation priorities, follow-up process,
review strategy, content direction, and local visibility improvements.

## Phase 4: Implementation

Build and launch the agreed growth assets, systems, campaigns, content,
communication processes, and reporting structure.

## Phase 5: Growth Tracking

Track progress, review results, identify what is working, and adjust the growth
plan over time.

## Client Workflow

Every client should have their own workspace under:

```text
clients/
```

Each client folder should support the full growth process, beginning with
Business Discovery and expanding into analysis, blueprint creation,
implementation, and tracking.
